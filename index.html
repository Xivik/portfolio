About Me

I am Patrick Theijink, 30 years old and living in Goor. Overijssel/the Netherlands.

I am an aspiring Web Developer, I'd dabbled in web development over the years (mostly just HTML static web pages) but I never took it any further. When I took Computer Science in High School we learned a bit of Java, however as a 15 year old it was incredibly abstract and I barely managed to scrape by.

And that feeling stuck with me over the years. Everytime I thought about IT and start developing my skills there was this voice in the back of my head: "Remember that java course? You can't code!"

The turning point came when I was looking for a challenge, work wasn't challenging me and I felt a strong urge to learn something new, I felt like I was stuck in place and not getting anywhere, nor was I getting any satisfaction from the work I was doing.

So I set out to finally beat that old ghost that kept haunting me everytime I thought about coding. "I can't code? We'll see about that!" After looking at the options I landed on JavaScript. Now I just needed my first challenge.

I soon found one, I currently work as a Customer Serivce agent for the Dutch Tax and Customs Administration. As you can imagine, you deal with payments, a lot. However a lot of the calculations for the payment plans are done by hand. Ex. A customer calls and asks about the status of their payment plan. Are they behind? If so, by how much? Did they build up a lead so they can skip the payment this month?

Those questions had to be answered by calculating the payment plan by hand and working out the current status of the customer. Not a hard job, but time consuming and the chance of a simple miscalculation was always there.

After developing a tool to do it with JavaScript (check it out here) I was hooked. I went for it and here I am, learning and growing day by day and absolutely loving it.

I finally found that drive and passion that is so important in life and I can't wait to learn more and get better, one day at a time.
